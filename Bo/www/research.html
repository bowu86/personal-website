Bo Wu – Research

Algorithmic Foundation for Trusted and Proactive Autonomy

My research develops theories, algorithms, and tools necessary to answer the following central question to realize the full spectrum of societal and industrial benefits of future autonomy “how can we, in a data-rich world, efficiently design and operate autonomous systems that can be trusted?”

Broadly speacking, the autonomous systems should

  1. provide verifiable performance in uncertain and dynamic environments,

  2. be resilient against corruption by adversaries,

  3. keep sensitive data from different sources private during system design and operation, and

  4. be proactive in achieving the desired goal in a timely manner.
